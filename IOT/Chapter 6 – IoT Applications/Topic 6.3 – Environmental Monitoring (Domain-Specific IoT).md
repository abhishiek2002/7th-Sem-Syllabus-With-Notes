# **Topic 6.3 – Environmental Monitoring (Domain-Specific IoT)**

This is a straightforward but very important IoT application.
Exams commonly ask:

* “Explain IoT in environmental monitoring.”
* “Write short notes on environmental monitoring using IoT.”
* “How can IoT help in environmental protection?”

Let’s keep it crisp and exam-ready.

---

# **1. What is Environmental Monitoring?**

Environmental Monitoring uses IoT sensors and communication technologies to **measure, track, and analyze environmental parameters** in real time.

In simple words:

> **IoT helps monitor pollution, weather, soil conditions, and natural environment to improve safety and sustainability.**

---

# **2. Key Components Used**

### **1. Sensors**

* Air quality sensors (CO2, PM2.5, NO2)
* Temperature & humidity sensors
* Soil moisture sensors
* Water quality sensors (pH, turbidity)
* Rain/precipitation sensors
* Noise sensors

### **2. Connectivity**

LoRaWAN, GSM, Wi-Fi, 4G/5G.

### **3. Cloud Platforms**

Used to store and analyze environmental data.

### **4. Dashboards & Apps**

Used by government and disaster management teams.

---

# **3. Major Applications of IoT in Environmental Monitoring**

This section is where you score marks. Mention at least 4–5.

---

## **(i) Air Quality Monitoring**

Sensors detect:

* PM2.5
* PM10
* CO2
* Smoke
* Toxic gases

Data is sent to cloud → live pollution index → alerts issued.

**Used in:** smart cities, schools, industries.

---

## **(ii) Water Quality Monitoring**

Measures:

* pH
* turbidity
* dissolved oxygen

Used in:

* rivers
* lakes
* pipelines
* wastewater treatment

---

## **(iii) Weather Monitoring**

IoT stations track:

* temperature
* rainfall
* wind speed
* humidity

Useful for agriculture, disaster forecasting, and climate studies.

---

## **(iv) Soil Monitoring**

Sensors measure:

* soil moisture
* soil temperature
* nutrients

Helps farmers decide irrigation and fertilization.

---

## **(v) Forest Fire Detection**

Temperature + smoke + gas sensors detect early fire signs.

Alerts help prevent large-scale forest fires.

---

## **(vi) Flood Monitoring**

Water level sensors monitor river height.

Real-time alerts help disaster management teams act early.

---

## **(vii) Noise Monitoring**

Noise sensors installed in cities measure sound pollution.

Used for traffic planning and urban safety.

---

# **4. Benefits of IoT in Environmental Monitoring**

### **1. Real-Time Data**

Immediate detection of environmental changes.

### **2. Early Warning Systems**

Fire, flood, air pollution alerts.

### **3. Better Decision Making**

Governments and farmers get actionable insights.

### **4. Automation**

Systems can automatically:

* start water pumps
* trigger alarms
* send notifications

### **5. Remote Monitoring**

Useful for remote locations like forests, mountains, mines.

---

# **5. Challenges**

Keep these short.

* Sensor calibration issues
* Device maintenance in harsh environments
* Connectivity problems in remote areas
* High deployment cost
* Data accuracy problems
* Power supply issues

---
