# Internet of Things (IoT) – Course Syllabus 

## **1. Introduction** 
**(Importance: Low–Medium)**
### **Objectives** 

* Understand the fundamentals and evolution of the Internet of Things.
* Explore how IoT integrates with modern computing systems.
* Identify real‑world applications and industry use‑cases.

### **Scope** 

* Covers IoT concepts, hardware, software, architectures, protocols, and major application domains.

### **Expected Outcomes** 
* Ability to explain IoT architecture and technologies.
* Hands‑on understanding of IoT hardware and OS.
* Knowledge of IoT‑Cloud‑Data integration.
* Understanding of challenges and future trends.
