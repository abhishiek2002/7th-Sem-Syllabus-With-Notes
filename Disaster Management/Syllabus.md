## **2. Understanding Disaster**

**(Importance: Very High — Core theory & long-answer questions come mainly from here)**

### Topics:

* Concept of disaster
* Different approaches to understanding disasters
* Concept of risk
* Levels of disasters
* Disaster phenomena and events (global, national, regional)

### **Hazards & Vulnerabilities**

* Natural & man-made hazards
* Response time, frequency, forewarning levels
* Characteristics & damage potential of natural hazards

### **Types of Disasters**

* Floods, cyclones, lightning, thunderstorms
* Hailstorms, avalanches, droughts
* Cold/heat waves, epidemics, pest attacks
* Forest fire, chemical, industrial, radiological, nuclear disasters
* Building collapse, rural/urban fire
* Road and rail accidents
